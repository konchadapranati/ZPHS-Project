Z. P. High School‚ Marupalli

In various educational schools from Vizianagaram the contribution of Z. P. Highschool, Marupalli is remarkable and praiseworthy. #Stay Home...#Stay Safe...

The school has 42 years glorious history. It is one of the oldest and pioneer school in Marupalli,Gajapathinagaram Mandal,VZM District. Till now,12 Headmasters worked a lot for the development of the school and still working. The school is a complete educational hub and surrounds of Model School,KGBV,PHC. The school is co-educational school of strength 350 members and has nearly 4000 successful passedouts. Among these 4000 members,many of them are into public sector jobs and many are police officers. And also many students are selected for many National wide tests and are successful. The school is surrounded with greenery all around and is very tidy and also marked as best school with best infrastructure.

The school is doing honest attempts to develope the moral values‚ to make them civilized citizens of this pious nation, to enter in new and modern world.
